Luxury Property Lead Generation

123 Qualified Leads

For a ~$933,000 USD Property (14 Milyar IDR)

My Role: B2C Funnel Strategist & Meta Ads Manager

The Challenge

A high-end Travel Agent client looked to diversify their assets by selling a luxury, high-ticket property. They had no prior brand presence in real estate and needed to generate high-intent leads from scratch.

The Goal

The primary KPI was to generate over 100 qualified leads for the ~$933k property, while maintaining an efficient Cost Per Lead (CPL) below $2.00 USD.

The Blueprint

We deployed a "High-Intent Audience Funnel" strategy, focusing on capturing a niche audience with high net worth and interest in luxury goods.

The Execution Funnel

1. Acquisition (Meta Ads)

Ran targeted Meta Ad campaigns to a custom audience interested in luxury goods, high-end travel, and property investment.

↓

2. Engagement (Landing Page)

Ads directed traffic to a dedicated landing page showcasing the property's exclusive features and offering a detailed e-book.

↓

3. Nurturing (Lead Magnet)

Users submitted their contact information to download the "Luxury Property Guide," qualifying them as a new lead.

↓

4. Qualification (Sales)

Qualified leads were immediately passed to the client's sales team for personal follow-up and final qualification.

Lesson Learned

The 45-64 age demographic, particularly males, responded most effectively to the campaign. This segment not only absorbed the most budget but also correlated with the highest-intent leads. Future campaigns for similar high-ticket items should be weighted heavily towards this proven demographic.
