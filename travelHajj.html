Final Analysis & Strategic Takeaways

The "Community Trust" funnel was a resounding success, beating the CPL target by 18% and achieving a final CPL of $0.41. More importantly, the campaign generated a staggering 49.42X ROAS, proving the leads were not only cheap but also highly valuable.

Key Finding: A Full-Funnel Success Story

This campaign's true strength is its performance across the entire funnel. From 1,071 total leads, we successfully nurtured 350 into conversations, which the sales team converted into 10 in-person meetings, ultimately closing 2 high-value deals. This demonstrates a seamless transition from digital marketing to real-world sales.

Key Finding: Authenticity is the Ultimate Conversion Driver

The data speaks for itself. The 'Darussalam' series (Poster & Carousel), featuring authentic customer testimonials, generated 750 leads (70% of total). In stark contrast, the generic 'Adn Carousel' produced only 100 leads (9.3% of total). This proves that for high-trust purchases, social proof is not just a nice-to-have; it's the core of the strategy.

Benchmark vs. Meta Travel Standards

  • Link CTR: Our best creative (Darussalam Carousel) achieved a 1.56% CTR, significantly outperforming the Meta travel benchmark of 0.90% - 1.20%.
  • Cost Per Lead (CPL): Our $0.41 CPL is exceptionally low for the travel sector, demonstrating hyper-efficient targeting and an irresistible offer.
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): A 49.42X ROAS is virtually unheard of. It far exceeds the "good" benchmark of 3-5X, showcasing the immense profitability of this approach.

Market Context: Indonesia

This campaign was executed in the Indonesian market, which typically has lower media costs. The $0.41 CPL reflects this. However, the strategy, 70/30 creative split, 32.7% engagement rate, and 49.42X ROAS are universally applicable insights into the power of community trust and authentic marketing.

Strategic Takeaway: For high-ticket services, prioritize building trust with authentic social proof over generic branding. The quality of leads, evidenced by the progression from digital clicks to in-person meetings and a massive ROAS, is the ultimate measure of success.
