The Challenge

A Property Developer company needed to quickly and cost-effectively recruit a team of property agents in Indonesia. The primary barrier was achieving a low cost-per-lead (CPL) for a niche B2B audience.

The Goal

The main objective was to generate a high volume of qualified leads (potential agents) while maintaining a strict target CPL of under $0.33 (5,000 IDR) to ensure a high return on ad spend (ROAS) for the recruitment budget.

Strategy & Tools

We implemented a "Lean Tech-Stack Funnel" strategy. This approach minimized technical overhead, focusing on a rapid, data-driven path from ad exposure to lead capture.

Meta Ads Performance: Deep Dive

This CPL was achieved through superior ad performance. The data shows clear winners in ad creative and audience segments, proving the campaign's efficiency against industry averages (Est. CTR ~1.5%, Est. CPC $0.15/2,250 IDR).

Lead Experience Level

A key question was the experience level of the leads. The campaign successfully attracted a large base of new agents, identifying a major opportunity for the client to focus on training and development.

Digital Marketing Readiness

We also assessed their current marketing activity. An overwhelming 80% of leads were already "digitally-literate," using platforms like WA, IG, or TikTok, making them ideal candidates for a modern, digital-first agency.

Lesson Learned & Key Insight

The campaign was an outstanding success, achieving an ultra-low CPL 54% below the target. The most valuable insight was the lead composition: 72% of leads are new to the industry. This allowed the client to pivot their onboarding strategy from simple recruitment to a "New Agent Training Program," dramatically increasing retention and long-term value.
