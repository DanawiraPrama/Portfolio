Executive Summary

How a minimal budget campaign achieved exceptional results in property agent recruitment

With a minimal budget of only $67 USD, our Meta Ads campaign successfully recruited over 20 qualified property agents in just 6 days. The campaign utilized a strategic webinar approach with the theme "How to Generate Income from Property" to attract potential candidates.

The campaign achieved an impressive 80.4% conversion rate from form submissions to WhatsApp group joins, with over 10% of those ultimately signing cooperation agreements to become official property agents.
