Analysis & Lessons Learned

The campaign successfully exceeded the primary goal, acquiring over 500 leads while staying on target for the $0.33 CPL. The 6.40% CTR was exceptionally high, indicating that the ad creatives and value proposition strongly resonated with the target audience. The low $1.60 CPM confirms the efficiency of the audience targeting.

Lesson Learned: The data showed that the "Araz Ad Set 31 Oct" performed well, but a significant portion of the budget was allocated to "Other Ad Sets" (which included testing). Future campaigns can be optimized by reallocating more budget to proven high-performing ad sets earlier in the process.
