Campaign Analysis & Insights

Targeting Strategy

Focused on high-value demographics: professionals aged 35-54 with demonstrated interest in luxury properties and investment opportunities.

Ad Format Performance

Video ads generated 40% more engagement than static images, while carousel ads drove the highest click-through rates at 7.2%.

Timing Optimization

Weekend campaigns performed 65% better than weekday campaigns, with Saturday afternoons showing peak engagement.
